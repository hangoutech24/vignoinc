Our Training Philosophy

At Vigno, we focus on quickly teaching you real-world IT skills you can use immediately. Seasoned experts and constantly updated courseware combine with real-world lab exercises to challenge your understanding of the technology.

We don't rely on mindless click-to-complete labs or rote memorization. Our labs present you with real problems you'll be faced with on the job. They incorporate real variables and challenge your problem solving capabilities. You get the most realistic experience possible so you'll be ready to tackle similar issues back at the office.
